- **开盘价特征 (Opening Price Profile)**
  - *Field*: `open_price_gap` (高开/低开幅度)
  - *Description*: Magnitude of gap-up/down versus previous close. Bottom-range high openings suggest aggressive accumulation; high-range high openings suggest distribution. Contextualized by stock's position in trend cycle (底部/拉升/高位).

- **开盘成交量与换手率 (Opening Volume & Turnover)**
  - *Field*: `opening_volume_ratio` (开盘5/15分钟量比)
  - *Description*: Volume in first 5-15 minutes compared to 20-day average. New IPOs require tracking `listing_day_turnover` (>60% indicates heavy accumulation). Stealth accumulation shows subtle volume increases at channel bottoms with contraction at tops.

- **盘口挂单结构 (Order Book Structure)**
  - *Field*: `large_order_dynamics` (大单挂单特征)
  - *Description*: Presence of large sell orders (大卖盘) during price rises tests market absorption; absence of large buy orders (无大承接盘) during declines indicates controlled selling. Watch for large orders on ask-side (sell level 1) vs small orders on bid-side (buy level 1) to mask true intent.

  - *Field*: `price_movement_naturalness` (走势自然度)
  - *Description*: Mechanical/stagnant movements indicate incomplete control; smooth, active trading suggests established control. Measured by tick-by-tick continuity and spread compression.

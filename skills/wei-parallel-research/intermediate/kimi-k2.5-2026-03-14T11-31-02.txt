To determine whether major players (主力资金) are accumulating a specific A-share stock during the morning opening session and identify their accumulation strategy, the following specific data fields and indicators are required. === Key Points ===
- **Opening Price Gap (开盘价涨跌幅)**: The percentage difference between today's opening price and yesterday's close. High opens (>2-3%) or low opens (<-2%) in specific price zones (bottom range vs. high range) indicate different strategies: high opens in bottom ranges suggest rapid accumulation/shakeouts, while high opens at highs suggest distribution [fupanwang.com](https://www.fupanwang.com/zhishi/8537.html).
- **Pre-market Auction Volume & Order Distribution (集合竞价成交量与挂单分布)**: The volume transacted during the call auction (9:15-9:25) and the balance between buy/sell orders at upper/lower price limits. Abnormally high auction volume with large sell orders at opening suggests testing pressure or controlled accumulation [fupanwang.com](https://www.fupanwang.com/zhishi/4190.html).
- **Opening Volume Ratio (开盘量比)**: The ratio of current trading volume to recent average volume. A significantly elevated ratio (>2-3x) at open with controlled price movement indicates active institutional participation [fupanwang.com](https://www.fupanwang.com/zhishi/1826.html).
- **Level-1/L2 Order Book Characteristics (盘口挂单特征)**: Specific observations of large orders on Sell Level 1 (卖一档大单) versus smaller orders on Buy Level 1 (买一档小单), and the presence/absence of large support orders during initial declines. Large sell orders during rises with gradual absorption indicate controlled accumulation; lack of support during drops suggests washout tactics [fupanwang.com](https://www.fupanwang.com/zhishi/6409.html).
- **Initial 5/15/30-Minute Candlestick Patterns (开盘初期K线形态)**: Whether the price forms "golden channel" patterns, immediate long bullish candles breaking resistance, or washout patterns (sharp declines with shrinking volume). A long bullish candle breaking through consolidation zones on high volume confirms accumulation completion [fupanwang.com](https://www.fupanwang.com/zhishi/3435.html).
- **Turnover Rate (换手率)**: For new listings or recent IPOs, the turnover rate during opening sessions. New stocks with >50-70% turnover in first days indicate major players targeting for position building [fupanwang.com](https://www.fupanwang.com/zhishi/3435.html).
- **Price Zone Context (股价所处区间)**: Whether the stock is in bottom accumulation, uptrend, or distribution phase. Same opening behavior (e.g., high open) has opposite meanings in different zones—accumulation in bottoms, distribution at highs [fupanwang.com](https://www.fupanwang.com/zhishi/8537.html).
